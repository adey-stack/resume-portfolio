Gesture-Controlled PC Interface
Featured · Personal

A real-time hand-tracking system that replaces the mouse entirely — move your index finger to move the cursor, pinch to click, hold the pinch to drag, and make a fist to instantly pause control. Built with MediaPipe's 21-point hand landmark model, tuned with a moving-average smoother and a deadzone filter to kill hand-shake jitter, and a pinch threshold that scales with hand size so it works at any distance from the camera.
